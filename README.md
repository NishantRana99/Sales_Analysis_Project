# Sales_Analysis_Project

## Problem Statement

You have been provided with a sales dataset from **Ferns and Petals (FNP)**, a company that specializes in sending gifts for various occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine’s Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

The dataset contains information related to:
- Products
- Orders
- Customers
- Order and delivery dates

The objective of this project is to analyze the dataset to uncover meaningful insights related to **sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance**, and to build a dashboard that helps the business improve sales strategy and customer satisfaction.

---

##  Business Questions Solved

The analysis answers the following key business questions:

1. What is the **total revenue** generated?
2. What is the **average order and delivery time**?
3. How do **monthly sales fluctuate** across the year 2023?
4. Which are the **top products by revenue**?
5. What is the **average customer spending**?
6. How do the **top 5 products perform in terms of sales**?
7. Which are the **top 10 cities by number of orders**?
8. Does **order quantity impact delivery time**?
9. How does **revenue vary across different occasions**?
10. Which products are the **most popular among customers**?

    ##  Key Insights

- The business generated strong overall revenue, indicating healthy demand for online gifting across multiple occasions.

- Monthly sales showed clear seasonality, with higher revenue during major festive and celebration months such as Valentine’s Day, Raksha Bandhan, and Diwali.

- A small group of products contributed a significant portion of total revenue, highlighting the importance of top-performing products in driving sales.

- The top 5 products consistently outperformed others, making them key focus areas for inventory planning and promotions.

- Customers, on average, spent a moderate amount per order, indicating opportunities for upselling and bundle offers to increase average order value.

- Certain cities dominated the order volume, suggesting strong regional demand and potential locations for targeted marketing campaigns.

- Orders with higher quantities tended to have slightly longer delivery times, indicating operational pressure during bulk or high-volume orders.

- Occasion-based analysis revealed that festive and relationship-driven occasions generated higher revenue compared to regular days.

- Product popularity analysis showed that customers preferred specific gift categories, which can help optimize product assortment and marketing strategies.
